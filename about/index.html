Hi there! Welcome to my photo collection.


My interest in photography started when I found an old polaroid camera at my parents' office. Later on, I got my own camera and since then I have been capturing what comes to my eye. I have a special interest in photographing people, food and city life. All photos are taken with a Canon EOS 1000D or an Iphone 8.
